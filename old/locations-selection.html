Locations wanted for our Healthy Boost Kiosk

Minimum Requirements for Spaces Requested

  • Minimum Square Feet: 16 feet x10 feet
  • Minimum Backroom Storage: 200 square feet
  • Equipment needs vary per site conditions
  • No plumbing hook up necessary. We use portable sinks and will need to change out water periodically
  • lectricity will be connected with a power strip

One component of Healthy Boost overall growth strategy is non-traditional development. We are looking for locations in the following venues:

  • Airports
  • College and University campuses
  • Highway travel plazas
  • Hospitals
  • Military bases
  • Office buildings
  • Entertainment venues

These high-visibility, high-traffic locations offer convenient access customers. Developers of the various venues understand that adding a Healthy Boost to their location provides a diverse menu with a variety of Healthy Juice and fruit bowl options.

If you have any locations that you feel would be a great fit for Healthy Boost, please contact us: Realestate@Healthyboost.life
