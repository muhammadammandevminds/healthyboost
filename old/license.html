Initial Investment (for development of a new Juice Bar)

Development costs for a new kiosk can vary based on several factors, including but not limited to: real estate costs, building size and design features, local labor and building supplies, and permitting. The initial investment for building a traditional Juice Bar unit in the U.S. is estimated between $153,100 and $300,000. Healthy Boost also offers smaller formats and kiosks for non-traditional venues that can be developed at substantially lower costs. Kiosk prices range from $65,000 to $80,000 for fully equipped kiosks.

What support will I receive after initial training is completed?

One of the advantages of licensing with us is access to our support network. This support includes:

  • Vendor Relationships: We have established relationships with vendors who will guide you to the right products, equip ment, and small wares. We have also negotiated volume discounts on your behalf and pass all of those savings on to you.
  • Regional Support:After signing your licensing agreement, you will be in touch with your Regional Sales Manager, who will directly support you. They will provide operational input and best practices and visit you regularly to ensure we meet your needs and help you maximize your business potential.
  • Healthy Boost Support Center: On an ongoing basis, the Healthy Boost Support Center will drive the brand and support your efforts in marketing, real estate and development, supply chain, and IT. If you have questions or need assistance in these areas, your Regional Sales Manager will connect you with the appropriate team member at the support center
